Baby Development Tracker

Month by Month Development

Month 1

Size: About the size of a poppy seed

Development: The fertilized egg implants in the uterus and begins to develop into an embryo.

Month 2

Size: About the size of a kidney bean

Development: Major organs and systems begin to form, including the heart, nervous system, and digestive system.

Month 3

Size: About the size of a plum

Development: The baby's face and body become more defined, and the baby can move its arms and legs.

Month 4

Size: About the size of an avocado

Development: The baby's organs continue to develop, and the baby can now hear sounds outside of the womb.

Month 5

Size: About the size of a mango

Development: The baby's movements become more coordinated, and the baby begins to develop sleeping and waking patterns.

Month 6

Size: About the size of a papaya

Development: The baby's lungs are developing, and the baby can now respond to sounds and light.

Month 7

Size: About the size of a pineapple

Development: The baby's eyes can now open and close, and the baby's brain is rapidly developing.

Month 8

Size: About the size of a honeydew melon

Development: The baby is growing rapidly and putting on weight, and the baby's bones are fully formed.

Month 9

Size: About the size of a watermelon

Development: The baby is fully developed and ready for birth.
